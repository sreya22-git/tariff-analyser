What this tool solves

Tariff rates change all the time, and a single rate change on one HS code from one country can quietly eat into your margins before anyone notices. This tool reads your procurement data and shows you where your tariff exposure actually lives: which countries, which categories, and which purchase orders are costing you the most right now. Then it helps you decide what to do about each one.
